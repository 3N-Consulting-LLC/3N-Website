Meet Our Team

David O. Mann

Advisory Board Member

EOS Chairman | Venture Capital & Private Equity Leader | Entrepreneurial Strategy Expert | Former U.S. Navy Officer

Biography

David Mann is an entrepreneurship and growth strategy leader with 25+ years across venture capital, private equity, company-building, and executive education. He serves as Chairman of the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) and is Managing Partner & Co-Founder of both The Firefly Group and Spring Mill Venture Partners. David has sourced, structured, and guided dozens of investments across technology, life sciences, and services, serving on boards and coaching CEOs to translate strategy into repeatable revenue, operating discipline, and measurable results.

A former U.S. Navy Supply Corps Officer and White House Liaison to the Secretary of the Navy, David brings operational rigor and mission focus to every engagement. For nearly two decades, he taught in Indiana University's Kelley School of Business MBA program, where he designed and led the Venture Capital & Entrepreneurial Finance course, and he has guest-lectured at Purdue and Notre Dame.

Education & Background

David holds an MBA from Harvard Business School (with additional coursework at the Kennedy School) and a B.S. from the U.S. Naval Academy, where he served as Company Commander and on the Brigade Honor Committee.
