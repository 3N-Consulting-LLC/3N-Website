Nick Sickler

Advisory Board Member & Interim Chief Financial Officer

CPA | 24+ Years of Accounting & Financial Leadership | Finance Operations & Governance Expert

Biography

Nick Sickler serves as 3N Consulting's Interim Chief Financial Officer and Advisory Board Member, bringing more than two decades of accounting and financial leadership experience. Nick currently serves as Chief Financial Officer at Great Point Capital, where he oversees finance operations and leads the firm's accounting department. A Certified Public Accountant, Nick's career spans over 24 years in public accounting, working with privately held businesses and high-net-worth individuals to help them achieve their financial goals through sound audit, tax, and trust planning practices.

Before joining Great Point Capital, Nick spent nearly 25 years at John R. Waters & Company, a respected Chicago-based public accounting firm, where he advanced to Attestation Manager and earned a reputation for delivering high-quality client service. Deeply engaged in professional and civic life, he has held leadership roles with the Purdue Club of Chicago, the Purdue Alumni Association, and the Risk Management Association's Chicago Chapter, where he served as both President and Treasurer.

Education & Professional Credentials

Nick holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from Purdue University and is an active member of both the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA) and the Illinois CPA Society. His hands-on leadership, financial acumen, and long-standing dedication to integrity and excellence strengthen 3N Consulting's fiscal strategy and governance as the firm continues its growth across Maryland and beyond.
