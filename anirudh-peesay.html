Anirudh ("Anu") Peesay

Dedicated professional and valued member of the 3N Consulting team.

About Anu

Anu Peesay is the Assistant to the CEO at 3N Consulting, where he supports organizational strategy through data analysis, programming, and the application of mathematical modeling to business challenges. He brings a structured, analytical approach to problem-solving and plays a key role in developing internal systems, documentation, and technical processes that strengthen operational efficiency.

At 3N, Anu contributes to firmwide initiatives that enhance digital infrastructure and knowledge management. He is currently leading the design and deployment of the 3N Consulting Group website, ensuring that the firm's online presence reflects its mission, capabilities, and client-centered approach. His work emphasizes clarity, technical precision, and an evidence-based mindset that supports informed decision-making across projects.

Background & Expertise

A Maryland native, Anu holds a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics with a concentration in Mathematical Biology from the University of Pittsburgh. His academic background shapes his ability to transform complex quantitative and qualitative information into clear insights that guide organizational priorities and illuminate areas for strategic improvement.

Core Competencies:

Data analysis and visualization, programming, research synthesis, mathematical modeling, documentation, and technical problem-solving.
