Marc Hochman

Advisory Board Chair

Partner Emeritus at A.T. Kearney | 24+ Years of Leadership | Digital Analytics & Operations Expert

Biography

Marc Hochman was appointed Partner Emeritus with A.T. Kearney after spending more than 24 years with the firm in a variety of leadership roles. In addition to client work across a diverse portfolio of industries (Automotive, Consumer Products and Retail, Energy & Process Industries, Financial Institutions, and Healthcare), Marc most recently served as Chief Operating Officer of Kearney Digital & Analytics. Prior to taking on that role, he co-founded and co-led Kearney Analytics and was President of Cervello, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Kearney that serves as a trusted advisor to blue-chip clients, earning a stellar reputation as a market leader in agile analytics, CRM, enterprise performance management, data management, and business intelligence.

Before being named President of Cervello, Marc served as Chief Operating Officer for A.T. Kearney Solutions, was a member of the Americas Leadership Team, and led the firm's global Staffing & Operations function. Additionally, Marc chaired the firm's US Investment Committees for over a decade. Prior joining A.T. Kearney, Marc spent 12 years working for Air Products & Chemicals and American Cyanamid where he held positions in cost engineering, applied research & development, energy management, finance, and procurement.

Education & Professional Leadership

Marc holds a B.S. in Chemical Engineering and M.S. in Management (Strategy and Finance), both from Purdue University. He served as President of the Krannert School Board of Directors from 2013-2019, was a member of the Dean's Advisory Council from 2020-2025, and is a member of Purdue's President's Council. His extensive background in operations, analytics, and strategic leadership brings invaluable perspective to 3N Consulting's governance and strategic direction.
